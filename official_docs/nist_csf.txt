NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0
Country: USA
Official source: https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework

[Summary]
The NIST CSF provides a framework for managing and reducing cybersecurity risk in public and private organizations. It is based on five core functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.

[Functions]
- Identify
- Protect
- Detect
- Respond
- Recover
