National Security Framework (ENS) - Spain
Country: Spain
Official source: https://www.boe.es/buscar/act.php?id=BOE-A-2010-13317

[Summary]
The ENS establishes the principles and minimum requirements for information protection in the Spanish public sector.

[Key principles]
- Information protection
- Risk management
- Security categories
- Security measures
