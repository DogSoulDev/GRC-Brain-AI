General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
Country: EU
Official source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32016R0679

[Summary]
The GDPR regulates the protection of personal data in the European Union. Key principles: lawfulness, fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity and confidentiality, accountability.

[Main articles]
- Article 5: Principles relating to processing of personal data
- Article 6: Lawfulness of processing
- Article 7: Conditions for consent
- Article 32: Security of processing
- Article 33: Notification of personal data breaches
- Article 35: Data protection impact assessment
- Article 83: Penalties
