ISO/IEC 27001:2022 - Information Security Management Systems
Country: International
Official source: https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html

[Summary]
ISO/IEC 27001 is the international standard for information security management. It defines requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an ISMS.

[Key principles]
- Confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA triad)
- Risk assessment and treatment
- Security controls
- Continuous improvement
